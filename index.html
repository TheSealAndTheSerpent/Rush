The Seal and Serpent Society

The Seal and Serpent Society is Cornell's oldest independent fraternity, and is gender-neutral and only located at Cornell. Founded in 1905, we dedicate ourselves to build strong bonds between Cornell students, and host social events for the Cornell community. We invite you to join us at any of our Spring 2025 rush events at our lodge, located at 305 Thurston avenue.
